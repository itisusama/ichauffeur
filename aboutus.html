About Our Company

THE BRITISH STANDARD SINCE 2005

OUR HISTORY

__________

iChauffeur has developed over the last decade into one of the finest British chauffeur companies, and has become a name synonymous within the industry for professionalism, refinement and luxury. iChauffeur was founded in 2005, by the late Andrew Senior along with his son Will, and later on was joined by his eldest son Richard. Starting with just two vehicles and a handful of exclusive clientele, today there are over 100 professional chauffeurs who drive some of the most important and influential people on the globe.
