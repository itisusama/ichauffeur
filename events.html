WE HANDLE THE LARGEST EVENTS

PLANNING FOR THE SMALLEST DETAILS

__________

Corporate events require meticulous planning and a professional executive transportation service you can rely on.

If there's one certainty about any aspect of events planning, it's the uncertainty. 'Last minute changes', 'Eleventh hour deviations', 'Late arrivals at the airport', 'Flooded roads'. You name it, our iChauffeur project managers have heard it, seen it and solved it.

Solving problems becomes easier with experience. And, quite frankly, there are few luxury event transportation firms that can boast the level of practical, hands-on proficiency that we have accumulated over the 15 years of iChauffeur's existence.

Our key project management team has combined experience of more than 50 years. So, going the extra mile is something they do intuitively. Every year, we chauffeur VIPs - the numbers of which can easily be in the hundreds - to between 75 and 100 major UK events.

Our chauffeurs are all the epitome of discretion, courtesy, reliability and professionalism. They have all gone through the most exacting evaluation process. They all have exceptional topographical skills (they know the lay of the land regarding your event because they reconnoitre routes before driving your guests). And, above all, they are all dedicated to caring for the VIP passengers with the upmost efficiency, and safety.
